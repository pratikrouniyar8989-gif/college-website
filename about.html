We reimagined British education for Kathmandu’s boldest learners.

Story

Born in Kathmandu, plugged into Sunderland.

ISMT British College started with a simple belief—international credentials should feel local, human, and future-facing. Today we operate as a hybrid innovation campus with direct validation from the University of Sunderland and Pearson BTEC.

Students build ventures, craft experiences, and prototype technology inside the same studios where visiting founders and faculty teach. Community impact, inclusivity, and employability anchor every decision.

Blueprint

Mission, vision, and the principles that keep us restless.

Mission

Deliver international degrees that feel immersive, industry-led, and accessible.

We design learning experiences where students think critically, collaborate with mentors worldwide, and graduate with a portfolio that employers trust.

Vision

Become South Asia’s most agile British campus.

By scaling research labs, expanding residencies, and fueling scholarships, we are building a community of creators shaping policy, business, and culture.

Principles
  • Learning happens through making.
  • Diversity of thinkers beats uniformity.
  • Every brief is a doorway to a real-world partner.
  • Careers are designed, not discovered.
Timeline

Moments that shaped our campus.

2011 · Campus launch

Opened with our first batch of Sunderland validated programs and a promise to keep education global yet attainable.

2016 · Studio expansion

Introduced the Experience Lab and hospitality suite so every assessment mirrors the workplace.

2021 · Dual-city semesters

Students now split time between Kathmandu and Sunderland/Dubai with unified credit transfer.

2024 · Applied research labs

Launched XR, climate impact, and fintech micro-labs funded by alumni and industry partners.
