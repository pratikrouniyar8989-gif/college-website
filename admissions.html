Admissions

We built a calm, transparent admissions journey that mirrors our studio culture.

Whether you are transferring credits, applying straight after high school, or returning for graduate study, the admissions team co-designs a path that fits your goals.

Journey

Four deliberate steps from application to onboarding.

01 · Share your story

Application & portfolio

Submit your transcripts, statement of intent, and optional creative or technical work. Tell us what you want to build.

02 · Aptitude

Studio challenge

Depending on the program, complete a short problem-solving task or entrance quiz. We look for curiosity, not perfection.

03 · Dialogue

Conversation + review

Meet faculty and career leads for a conversation about your goals. Documents are verified in parallel.

04 · Welcome

Offer & onboarding

Receive a personalized study map, scholarship decision, and onboarding kit. Orientation begins 4 weeks before classes.

Eligibility

Who we’re looking for.

We welcome ambitious learners from Nepal and abroad. The table below summarizes key requirements.

Undergraduate

  • 10+2 / A Levels with at least 60% aggregate.
  • Strong foundation in Math/English for tech programs.
  • IELTS 6.0 or equivalent for international applicants.

Postgraduate

  • Bachelor’s degree with minimum 50%.
  • Professional experience preferred for MBA.
  • IELTS 6.5 or equivalent for international applicants.

Scholarships

We reward bold ideas and community impact.

Merit, women-in-tech, and community impact scholarships cover up to 70% tuition. Submit a two-minute video or concept note outlining the impact you hope to make.

  • Merit: Based on academic excellence + studio challenge.
  • Women-in-Tech: For women pursuing STEM majors.
  • Impact: For projects tackling local challenges.
